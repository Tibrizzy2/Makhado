About Makhado

what is makhado ?

Makhado General Garage is a bustling hub of activity, situated in the heart of the vibrant town of Makhado. As you step inside, the air is filled with the scent of oil and gasoline, and the sound of engines humming fills your ears. This garage has become a trusted name in the community, known for its exceptional service and skilled mechanics.Makhado General Garage offers a wide range of services to cater to every customer's needs. From routine oil changes and tire rotations to complex engine repairs and diagnostics, they have the expertise to handle it all.
